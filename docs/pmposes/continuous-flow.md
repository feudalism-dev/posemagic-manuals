**Continuous Flow** means putting all your poses in one single \PMposes\ notecard.

### Pros
*   Easy to see everything at once.
*   Good for smaller furniture (1-4 sitters).

### Structure
\\\
# Pose: Sit
sitter 0: ...
sitter 1: ...
# Pose: Stand
sitter 0: ...
sitter 1: ...
\\\
